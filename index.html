About me

I am an illustrator working in the south of England. Most of my work starts with either traditional or digital pen and ink, and is then digitally coloured. The illustrations tend to take a slightly sideways look at the subject to provide a less obvious view point.

If you want to get in touch, pop along to my portfolio site or DM me on Mastodon.
